# Plans & Support — Northwind Cloud

## Plans
- **Free**: 5 GB storage, 1 user, community support.
- **Pro**: $12 per user / month, 1 TB storage, version history for 90 days,
  priority email support.
- **Business**: $22 per user / month, unlimited storage, SSO/SAML, audit logs,
  and a dedicated account manager.

You can switch plans at any time under Settings → Billing → Plan. Upgrades take
effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing
period.

## Support hours
Email support is available 9am to 5pm US Eastern, Monday through Friday. Pro and
Business plans receive priority response targets: Pro within one business day,
Business within four business hours.

## Data & storage
Files are encrypted at rest with AES-256 and in transit with TLS 1.2 or higher.
Data is stored in US and EU regions; you choose your region when you create the
workspace. We keep deleted files in the trash for 30 days before permanent
removal.

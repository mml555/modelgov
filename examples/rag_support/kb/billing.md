# Billing & Refunds — Northwind Cloud

## Refunds
Refunds are processed within 5 business days to the original payment method.
To request a refund, email billing@northwind.example within 30 days of the
charge. Annual plans are refundable on a pro-rata basis for the unused months.

## Failed payments
If a payment fails, we retry the card three times over 7 days. Your account
stays active during this grace period. After 7 days of failed retries the
account is downgraded to the Free plan; your data is retained for 60 days.

## Invoices
Invoices are available under Settings → Billing → Invoices. Each invoice can be
downloaded as a PDF. To add a VAT or tax ID to your invoices, enter it under
Settings → Billing → Company details.

## Currency and taxes
All prices are in USD. Sales tax or VAT is added at checkout based on your
billing country.

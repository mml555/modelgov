# Account & Security — Northwind Cloud

## Reset your password
Go to the sign-in page and click "Forgot password". We email a reset link that
is valid for 60 minutes. If the link expires, request a new one. For security,
resetting your password signs out all other active sessions.

## Two-factor authentication (2FA)
Enable 2FA under Settings → Security. We support authenticator apps (TOTP) and
hardware security keys. SMS codes are not supported. If you lose your device,
use one of the ten backup codes shown when you enabled 2FA.

## Change your email address
Update your email under Settings → Profile. A confirmation link is sent to the
new address; the change takes effect once you click it.

## Delete your account
Account deletion is under Settings → Danger Zone. Deletion is permanent after a
14-day cooling-off period, during which you can cancel by signing back in.

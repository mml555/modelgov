Welcome to Northwind Cloud

This is a stand-in "customer website." The support assistant in the bottom-right corner is the embeddable Ai-Guard RAG widget — it answers only from our help docs, cites its sources, and shows a per-reply governance receipt.

Try grounded questions (answered from the KB):

  • How long do refunds take?
  • How do I reset my password?
  • What does the Business plan cost?

Try an out-of-scope question (should be refused, not guessed):

  • Do you have a mobile app for Android?
  • What's your CEO's home address?

The widget is included with a single tag: <script src="/widget.js" data-endpoint="/api/chat"></script>
